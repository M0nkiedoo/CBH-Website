The Information Paradox, Fibre, and Spacetime Regularity

At the heart of black hole thermodynamics lies the information paradox. In CBH, this becomes: does the universe get enough fibre?

Information = Fibre:

Information helps transport and retain structure. Without enough of it, black holes accumulate unrelieved entropy.

White Holes as Information Restorers:

White holes may return scrambled quantum states—like waste still containing the trace of its source.

Spacetime Regularity:

Singularities are the breakdowns of structure—CBH suggests this is due to irregular information flow and lack of connective topologies.

Quantum Flatulence:

Hawking radiation may represent quantum burps—tiny bits of information escaping over long durations.
