4. The Information Paradox, Fibre, and Spacetime Regularity At the heart of black hole thermodynamics lies the information paradox. In CBH, this becomes: does the universe get enough fibre? 4.1. Information = Fibre: Information helps transport and retain structure. Without enough of it, black holes accumulate unrelieved entropy. 4.2. White Holes as Information Restorers: White holes may return scrambled quantum states—like waste still containing the trace of its source. 4.3. Spacetime Regularity: Singularities are the breakdowns of structure—CBH suggests this is due to irregular information flow and lack of connective topologies. 4.4. Quantum Flatulence: Hawking radiation may represent quantum burps—tiny bits of information escaping over long durations.
