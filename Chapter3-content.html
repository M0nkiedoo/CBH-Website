3. Activation Conditions for White Hole Ejection

One of the persistent issues with traditional white hole theory is that they appear to be mathematically valid but physically absurd. A permanent white hole would be: constantly ejecting matter, defying thermodynamic stability, and collapsing immediately. The CBH model resolves this by framing the white hole as an intermittent feature, not a static one.

White Holes as Pressure-Triggered Events

Matter is ingested by the black hole, compressed at or near the singularity, and held until a threshold condition is met—mass, entropy density, or spacetime curvature. At that point, the “cosmic sphincter” opens.

Loop Quantum Gravity and Bounce Cosmology

Certain quantum gravity models replace the singularity with a bounce, suggesting matter rebounds and emerges as an expanding white hole. CBH frames this as a pressure- release mechanism.

Biological Analogy

Biology shows that continuous intake without output is fatal. CBH echoes this: black holes consume, compress, and eventually expel. White holes are essential to cosmic health.

The Cosmic Constipation Hypothesis

If no white hole forms, Hawking radiation becomes the fallback—slow, inefficient, and comparable to cosmic indigestion.
