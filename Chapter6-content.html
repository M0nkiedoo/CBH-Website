Conclusion: A Final Movement

The Cosmic Bowel Hypothesis reframes the black hole-white hole cycle through a digestive metaphor. It is humorous, yes—but also pedagogically effective. It ties together intake, compression, entropy, pressure, and output in a form anyone with a body can understand.

Whether or not white holes exist, the CBH model makes one thing clear: Even the universe can't hold it in forever.
